## Copyright Assignment

By submitting a pull request, issue, or any other contribution to this
repository, you agree that:

1. You assign full copyright of your contribution to Murasaki Holdings
   Limited (Kenya).
2. Murasaki Holdings Limited may use, modify, sublicense, and distribute
   your contribution under any terms it chooses.
3. You have the right to make this assignment — the work is your own and
   is not subject to any conflicting agreement.

You retain no copyright or licence rights in your contribution after
submission. There is no CLA to sign — opening a pull request constitutes
your agreement to these terms.

## What We Accept

- Bug fixes
- Performance improvements
- New pieces or visual polish
- Game mode improvements

## What We Don't Accept

- Changes to the LICENSE or this file
- Dependencies that carry copyleft licences (GPL, AGPL, LGPL)

## How to Submit

1. Fork the repo
2. Make your changes on a branch
3. Open a pull request with a clear description of what and why
